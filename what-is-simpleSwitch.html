Overview Of simpleSwitch

simpleSwitch is an advanced power management system that allows you to connect high-energy appliances, like EV chargers and washers, to fully-loaded electrical panels without requiring costly upgrades.

Our technology monitors energy use in real time, balancing power between devices for seamless operation. Whether you're charging an electric vehicle or adding new appliances to your home, simpleSwitch ensures that your electrical system handles the load safely and efficiently.

Technical Specifications

  • Voltage Range: 120V-240V
  • Max Load Capacity: 80 Amps
  • UL-Listed
  • Real-Time Monitoring
  • Load Shedding Delay

Quick Answers To Common Questions

Guide to adding appliances with simpleSwitch

With simpleSwitch, adding appliances is quick and easy. Connect your device, open the app, and follow the intuitive setup process to control your appliances with a tap. Simplify home automation effortlessly.

Is Professional Installation Required for simpleSwitch?

simpleSwitch is designed for easy, DIY installation. Most users can set it up without professional help by following the step-by-step guide. However, for more complex setups, professional assistance is recommended.

Guide to adding EV chargers with simpleSwitch

Adding EV chargers with simpleSwitch is simple and convenient. Connect the charger to your home power supply, pair it with the simpleSwitch app, and follow the on-screen instructions to manage and monitor charging easily.

What Appliances Can I Add Using simpleSwitch?

With simpleSwitch, you can add a variety of appliances, including lights, fans, air conditioners, heaters, and home entertainment systems. It supports most smart home devices, offering seamless control and automation.

How to configure and use simpleSwitch?

To configure simpleSwitch, install the app, connect your appliance, and follow the setup steps to pair via Bluetooth or Wi-Fi. Control, schedule, and monitor appliances from the app for seamless management and energy savings.

How Does simpleSwitch Help Save on Energy Costs?

simpleSwitch helps save on energy costs by allowing you to control appliances remotely, schedule usage, and monitor energy consumption in real-time. By automating power management, it reduces unnecessary energy waste, optimizing efficiency.
